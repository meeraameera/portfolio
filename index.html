About Me

Hello, I’m Meera Ameera, an Applied AI graduate from Temasek Polytechnic. I’ve always been curious about how technology can solve real-world problems; through my academic projects and internship experience, I’ve learned how to turn that curiosity into practical solutions.

During my internship with the Data Engineering department at OCBC, I worked on automating data processing workflows and saw firsthand how system accuracy, reliability, and design choices have real business consequences. It’s been an eye-opening experience, and I’ve come to appreciate that building effective systems isn’t just about writing code that works—it’s about creating solutions that truly make a difference.

Currently, I am working at Stratova AI, an AI startup specializing in automation solutions. Here, I have gained exposure not only to AI solution development but also to outreach, marketing, sales, and digital transformation initiatives. This has given me a deeper understanding of how AI solutions are developed and applied in real-world settings.

Outside of work, I enjoy experimenting with AI and software engineering through personal projects, such as computer vision models and web-based applications. I also love running and traveling, which help me recharge, reflect, and gain fresh perspectives that I bring back to both my personal and professional work.
